Playing

Fri Jan 3 09:29:44 AM EST 2025

So, update from last night. I got the the store and saw four other people in the back ready to play and one was also a newbie. I got the autistic introductions from everyone and eventually was paired up with Kerry - a very put-together man who also really liked the game. He was very informative and soft-spoken and I had a great time listening to him explain everything. It was fun using my old minatures as "Scout Squad" proxies and people were actually super happy to see the old models. Some people even asked if I had the old set-pieces for the scenary (the broken ship from the old box). Super cool people and experience. I had a pretty good time, and I'm assuming that I'd like it more if I didn't have to ask Kerry about every move I made. After we played our match and I was introduced to a lot of the people, I left about 3 and a half hours later. I told Kerry it might be a while before I return, but I definitely want to go back to Gamer-Corps.
